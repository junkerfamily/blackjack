Dealer Peek Animation Demo

This sandbox isolates the dealer peek animation so you can preview the lift, scale, and glow effect without running a full game. Deal a peekable hand (Ace or 10-value upcard), trigger the animation, and adjust scenarios at will.

How it works:

  • Use “Force Blackjack” to guarantee an Ace/10 combination for quick visual testing.
